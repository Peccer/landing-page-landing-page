Does this scraper extract email addresses?

Yes. While Google Maps itself rarely displays email addresses, this scraper features a 'Website Crawler' mode that visits the business's linked website to find and extract email addresses and social media links.

How do I prevent getting blocked by Google?

You don't need to worry about that. The Google Maps Lead Extractor Pro runs on the Apify platform, which utilizes a sophisticated pool of residential and datacenter proxies to mask requests and prevent IP bans.

Can I integrate this with my existing software?

Absolutely. You can trigger this scraper programmatically via the Apify API and use Webhooks to push the resulting dataset directly to your database, Zapier, or CRM as soon as the job finishes.

What happens if the scraping stops or fails?

The Actor includes built-in state persistence. If a run is interrupted, it can resurrect and continue exactly where it left off, ensuring you don't lose data or pay for duplicate operations.

Is it possible to scrape specific geographic coordinates?

Yes. You can provide specific latitude and longitude coordinates to center your search, which is ideal for scraping strictly defined local areas or neighborhoods.

Cost depends on the volume of data and compute units used on the Apify platform. Because this Actor is optimized for performance, it is highly cost-effective compared to buying pre-made lead lists.
